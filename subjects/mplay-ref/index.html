What's in it for me?

At MyPlay, we cater to the discerning entertainment fan.


MyPlay powers more than 600 boutique shopping experiences with your favorite entertainment brands. From TV shows like Breaking Bad, to iconic artists like Michael Jackson, you won't find a more exciting selection of unique media and merch anywhere!

Let's get to know each other! Join MyPlay Recommends to receive custom updates tailored to your personal tastes. Whether new listener or vinyl fanatic, we'll help you fill out your collection while discovering new favorites.

We'll find you what you can't find elsewhere -- MyPlay stores contain access to exclusive pre-orders, one-of-a-kind products and limited edition releases. You'll be the first to know!
