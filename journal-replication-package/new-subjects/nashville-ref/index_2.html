Welcome to NashView, an easy-to-use map that helps you find out whats happening in your community! This tool offers a number of ways to see data thats important to you.

  • Regions Narrow down your view from the entire county to just your council district, school district, or ZIP code.
  • Places Find important locations such as police precincts, libraries, parks, and schools.
  • Pins Visualize incidents that affect you, including building permits, street and sidewalk improvements, and hubNashville requests.

Using NashView is easy. Try typing in your home or work address in the search box. Drag and zoom to find areas relevant to you. Create a free account to build and save custom searches and map views. Watch this video Guided Tour for a detailed look at what NashView can do.

We're always listening! Please direct questions, comments, and feedback to hub@nashville.gov.
